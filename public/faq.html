AgroConnect FAQ

Your Agriculture Knowledge Hub

🌾 Crop Management
What is crop rotation?
Crop rotation means growing different crops in sequence to improve soil fertility.
When is the best time to plant wheat?
Wheat is usually planted between October and November depending on region.
How can farmers increase crop yield?
Using quality seeds, proper irrigation, fertilizers, and pest management.
🧪 Soil Health
How often should I test my soil?
Soil testing is recommended every 2–3 years.
What does soil pH mean?
Soil pH indicates acidity or alkalinity affecting nutrient availability.
🐞 Pest Control
How do I identify common pests?
Look for leaf damage, holes, discoloration, and insect presence.
What is organic pest control?
Using natural solutions like neem oil or biological predators.
💧 Irrigation
How often should crops be watered?
Depends on crop type, soil moisture, and climate.
What is the most efficient irrigation system?
Drip irrigation is the most efficient system.
🚜 Equipment
What tools are essential for farming?
Tractors, ploughs, seed drills, irrigation pumps.
What is precision agriculture technology?
Technology using sensors and data for efficient farming.
