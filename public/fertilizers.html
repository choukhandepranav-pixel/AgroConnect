🌱 Fertilizer Recommendation

Urea

Urea is a nitrogen fertilizer used to increase plant growth and leaf development. It is widely used in rice and wheat farming.

Used for: Rice, Wheat, Maize

⭐⭐⭐⭐☆

DAP (Diammonium Phosphate)

DAP fertilizer provides nitrogen and phosphorus to crops. It helps roots grow stronger and improves plant development.

Used for: Rice, Cotton, Soybean

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

NPK Fertilizer

NPK fertilizer contains nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium. It supports overall plant growth and improves crop yield.

Used for: Wheat, Vegetables, Fruits

⭐⭐⭐⭐☆

Potash (MOP)

Potash fertilizer strengthens plant stems and improves crop quality. It also increases resistance against plant diseases.

Used for: Cotton, Rice, Vegetables

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Zinc Sulphate

Zinc Sulphate helps prevent zinc deficiency in soil and improves plant metabolism. It increases crop productivity.

Used for: Rice, Wheat, Maize
